OUR CORE VALUE

The effectiveness of ATAWAS in carrying out its diverse roles is underpinned by a set of core values that serve as the guiding principles for the organization. These values encapsulate the ethos and standards that ATAWAS upholds in its operations and interactions with stakeholders. The core values of ATAWAS a

  • Professionalism

    ATAWAS maintains professionalism, competence, ethics, and service excellence.

  • integrity

    Integrity drives ATAWAS's honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior.

  • objectivity

    Objectivity ensures ATAWAS's unbiased, impartial, and fact-based decision-making.

  • Transparency

    Transparency underpins ATAWAS's commitment to openness and accountability.
